FlashPublisher connects the Fichajes Flash content pipeline to TikTok, enabling scheduled publishing of original Spanish-language football analysis videos via the Content Posting API.

How FlashPublisher Works

1

Content Creation

Our pipeline generates original video scripts, voiceovers, and animated graphics for Spanish football analysis content.

2

Video Rendering

Videos are rendered locally using Remotion, producing 1080×1920 vertical videos with animated scenes and karaoke-style captions.

3

TikTok Publishing

FlashPublisher uses TikTok's Content Posting API to upload the rendered video with the appropriate title, description, and privacy settings.

About

FlashPublisher is developed by Abel Avilés as an internal tool for the Fichajes Flash TikTok channel (@fichajesflash). It is not a consumer-facing application — it serves as the publishing bridge between an automated content pipeline and TikTok's Content Posting API.

FlashPublisher uses the user.info.basic (Login Kit) scope to identify the authenticated Fichajes Flash account, and the video.upload (Content Posting API) scope to send original video content to that account. No other scopes are requested or used.
