Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FlashPublisher ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the FlashPublisher application and its integration with TikTok's Content Posting API.

FlashPublisher is developed and operated by Abel Avilés ("the Developer"). FlashPublisher is an internal publishing tool — it is not a consumer-facing application and does not serve end users other than the Developer.

1. What FlashPublisher Does

FlashPublisher is an automated video publishing tool that connects a content production pipeline to TikTok via the Content Posting API. It uploads original video content (Spanish-language football analysis) to a single TikTok account owned and operated by the Developer.

2. Information We Collect

FlashPublisher collects and processes the following information through its integration with TikTok:

  • TikTok OAuth tokens: Access tokens and refresh tokens obtained through TikTok's OAuth 2.0 authorization flow, used solely to authenticate video upload requests.
  • TikTok user profile information: Basic account information (display name, open ID) returned by TikTok during the OAuth flow, used to confirm the correct account is authorized.
  • Video upload metadata: Information associated with published videos, including titles, descriptions, and publication status returned by TikTok's API.

FlashPublisher does not collect personal information from any third-party users. The App interacts with a single TikTok account owned by the Developer.

3. How We Use Information

Information collected by FlashPublisher is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with TikTok's Content Posting API to upload videos.
  • Verify that the correct TikTok account is connected.
  • Track the publication status of uploaded videos.
  • Debug and maintain the publishing pipeline.

4. Data Storage and Security

OAuth tokens are stored locally on the Developer's machine in environment variable files that are excluded from version control. FlashPublisher does not transmit, share, sell, or make available any TikTok data to third parties.

All communication between FlashPublisher and TikTok's API occurs over HTTPS.

5. Data Sharing

FlashPublisher does not share, sell, rent, or disclose any data obtained from TikTok to any third party. Data collected from TikTok's API is used solely for the purposes described in Section 3.

6. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are retained only as long as they are valid and needed for the publishing function. Video metadata (titles, publish status) is retained in a local database for operational tracking and is not shared externally.

7. Children's Privacy

FlashPublisher is not designed for or directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). The App does not knowingly collect information from children.

8. Your Rights

Since FlashPublisher only processes data for the Developer's own TikTok account, no third-party user data is collected. If you believe your data has been processed in error, please contact us at the address below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
