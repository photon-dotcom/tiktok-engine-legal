FlashPublisher

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FlashPublisher ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information. FlashPublisher is a private internal publishing tool operated by the team behind Fichajes Flash. It is not a service offered to third parties.

1. Scope of this Policy

This Privacy Policy applies exclusively to FlashPublisher. Because FlashPublisher has no end users — it is used only by the Fichajes Flash operator to publish our own videos to our own TikTok accounts — this policy describes data handling as it applies to that single operator context.

2. Information FlashPublisher handles

FlashPublisher handles only the following data:

  • Video files we produce ourselves. FlashPublisher uploads MP4 files that the Fichajes Flash team has produced. These files are our own original content.
  • Captions, titles, and hashtags we write ourselves. These accompany the video when uploaded.
  • Basic profile information for our own authenticated TikTok account. Via the user.info.basic scope, FlashPublisher receives the open ID, avatar, and display name of the Fichajes Flash account that has been connected. This is used only to confirm which of our own accounts is authenticated.
  • OAuth access tokens for our own TikTok accounts. FlashPublisher stores an access token locally on our production machine to authenticate against the Fichajes Flash TikTok accounts.

FlashPublisher does not collect, store, or process personal data from any third party. FlashPublisher does not have end-user registration or sign-in.

3. Information FlashPublisher does not access

FlashPublisher uses only the user.info.basic (Login Kit) and video.upload (Content Posting API) scopes. Through these scopes, FlashPublisher accesses only the basic profile information of our own authenticated Fichajes Flash account (open ID, avatar, display name). FlashPublisher does not access, read, or store:

  • TikTok profile information for any account other than the Fichajes Flash accounts we operate
  • Email addresses, phone numbers, or other contact details
  • TikTok videos other than those we upload ourselves
  • Comments, likes, shares, or engagement data
  • Follower lists or follower information
  • Direct messages
  • Any data belonging to any TikTok user other than the Fichajes Flash operator account

4. How data is stored

The OAuth access tokens used by FlashPublisher are stored locally, on the production machine operated by the Fichajes Flash team. Tokens are not shared with any third party and are not transmitted to any server other than TikTok's official API endpoints.

Video files and captions uploaded via FlashPublisher are transmitted directly to TikTok's API using HTTPS. FlashPublisher does not store copies of uploaded content on any server other than the local production machine where the content was originally produced.

5. Third-party services

FlashPublisher communicates only with the official TikTok Content Posting API (developers.tiktok.com). No other third-party services receive data through FlashPublisher.

6. Data retention

OAuth access tokens are retained locally for as long as they remain valid and FlashPublisher continues to operate. Tokens can be revoked at any time through the TikTok account settings, which immediately disables FlashPublisher's ability to publish.

7. Children's privacy

FlashPublisher is an internal tool operated only by the Fichajes Flash team. It is not directed at, and does not knowingly interact with, children.

8. Changes to this Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
