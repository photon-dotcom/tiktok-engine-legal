Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how TikTok Engine ("the Service"), operated by Abel ("we", "us"), handles information in connection with its use of the TikTok API.

2. Information We Collect

The Service is an internal content publishing tool. We do not collect, store, or process any personal data from end users, viewers, or any third parties.

Specifically, the Service:

  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or contact information from users.
  • Does not use cookies, analytics, or tracking technologies.
  • Does not collect or access TikTok user data beyond our own accounts.
  • Does not store any data from TikTok viewers or commenters.

3. TikTok API Usage

The Service uses the TikTok Content Posting API solely to publish video content to TikTok accounts that we own and operate. We access the following TikTok API scopes:

  • video.publish — to upload and publish videos to our accounts.
  • video.upload — to upload video files.

We do not access any user data, follower lists, messages, or analytics of other TikTok users through the API.

4. Data Storage

The only data stored by the Service is:

  • Generated video files, audio files, and scripts (stored locally on our devices).
  • API authentication tokens for our own TikTok accounts (stored securely in environment variables, never committed to source code).

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose any personal data to third parties. The Service interacts with the following third-party APIs exclusively for content creation:

  • ElevenLabs — text-to-speech audio generation.
  • OpenAI Whisper — audio transcription for captions (processed locally).
  • TikTok API — video publishing.

6. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Since we do not collect personal data from users, there is no user data to retain or delete. Generated content (videos, audio, scripts) is retained locally at our discretion.

If you believe we hold any data related to you and wish to request its deletion, contact us at the address below.

8. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Request access to any personal data we may hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict the processing of your data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: abel@photon.com.es
